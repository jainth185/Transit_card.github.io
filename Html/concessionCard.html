Introduction

We offer a wide range of concession cards to students, national servicemen and senior citizens, just to name a few. Use them with the Monthly Concession Pass to enjoy more affordable fares on your next travel.

Student

Student Concession Card holders are entitled to purchase monthly concession passes to enjoy unlimited travel on basic buses and train services.

Children

The Child Concession Card is a stored value travel card that enables children to travel for free on public transport, except on Premium Bus Services and NightRider services.

Person With Disabilities

Persons with Disabilities (PWD) Concession Card holders can enjoy up to 55% off adult fares and do not have to pay additional fares for travel distances beyond 7.2km. They can also purchase monthly concession passes for unlimited travel on train and basic bus services.

Full-Time National Servicemen

With the NSF Concession Card or PAssion NSF Concession Card, full-time National Servicemen can purchase Monthly Concession Passes for unlimited travel on train and/or basic bus services.

Senior Citizens

This convenient stored value travel card is specially catered for senior Permanent Residents aged 60 years and above for hassle-free concessionary travel on buses, the MRT and LRT.
